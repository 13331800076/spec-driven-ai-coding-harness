# Requirement: CRM Lead Management

The system should provide a comprehensive lead management feature for the sales team.

Users should be able to create, update, assign, qualify, and convert leads to opportunities.

The system should support lead scoring based on engagement (email opens, website visits, form submissions).

The system should automatically assign leads to sales representatives based on territory, industry, or round-robin rules.

The system should record all lead interactions (calls, emails, meetings, notes) in an activity timeline.

The system should support duplicate detection and merging when a lead already exists in the system.

The system should enforce data validation: email must be valid and unique, phone must follow E.164 format.

The lead service should expose APIs for CRUD operations, search, scoring, assignment, and conversion.
